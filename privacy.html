Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2, 2025

Your Privacy Matters

This Privacy Policy describes how Free Time ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account using Apple Sign In, we collect:

  • Email address (either your real email or Apple's private relay email)
  • Unique user identifier for your account
  • Account creation and last login dates
  • Authentication tokens (securely stored and encrypted)

1.2 Usage and Analytics Data

To help you understand your digital habits and improve the App, we collect:

  • Time spent using blocked applications (distracted time)
  • Number of times you attempt to open blocked apps
  • Focus session durations and completion rates
  • Usage patterns by time of day
  • Puzzle completion statistics and preferences
  • Focus streaks and achievement milestones

1.3 App Configuration Data

  • Your app blocking preferences and group configurations
  • Blocking schedules and time restrictions
  • Notification preferences and puzzle type selections
  • Custom context labels you create
  • Daily intention text (if you choose to set one)

1.4 Technical Data

  • Device identifier (for data validation purposes only)
  • iOS version (for compatibility purposes)
  • App crashes and error reports (to improve stability)

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We are committed to minimal data collection. We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identifying information beyond email (name, phone number, address)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Content of your messages, emails, or other communications
  • Photos, videos, or other media files
  • Browsing history or website content
  • Contact lists or social media data

3. Third-Party Services We Use

3.1 Firebase by Google

  • Authentication: Secure user sign-in and account management
  • Firestore Database: Secure cloud storage for your account information
  • App Check: Device validation and security protection

3.2 Mixpanel

  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous tracking of app usage and feature interactions
  • Performance Metrics: App stability and error tracking
  • Anonymous Identification: Unique anonymous identifiers for tracking
  • No Personal Data: Only Firebase user IDs or anonymous device identifiers

Firebase and Mixpanel services are subject to their respective privacy policies. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

4. How We Store Your Data

4.1 Local Storage

Most data is stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage systems. This includes preferences, usage statistics, and blocking configurations.

4.2 Cloud Storage

Account information is securely stored in Firebase Firestore, including account email and authentication information.

4.3 Encryption and Security

  • AES-256-GCM encryption for sensitive data storage
  • iOS Keychain for secure token storage
  • Multi-location backup system with integrity validation

5. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the App's functionality:

  • Enable app blocking and focus session features
  • Provide usage insights and progress tracking
  • Maintain preferences across app launches
  • Improve app stability and performance
  • Synchronize data across devices (if iCloud sync enabled)

6. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is only shared with:

  • Firebase/Google: As our secure database and authentication provider
  • Apple's iCloud/CloudKit: Only if you enable iCloud sync
  • Apple's Screen Time framework: Required for blocking functionality

We do not use advertising networks or data brokers.

7. Your Rights and Controls

  • Data Access: View your usage analytics within the App
  • Data Deletion: Delete all local data by uninstalling the App
  • Account Deletion: Delete your account through the App's settings
  • iCloud Control: Enable or disable iCloud sync in device settings
  • Screen Time Control: Revoke permissions in iOS Settings

8. Data Retention

Local data is retained until you delete the App. iCloud data follows Apple's policies. Usage analytics are automatically purged after 365 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Free Time is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and through the App for significant changes.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy:

  • Through the App's support feature in Settings
  • By email at support@findfreetime.com

We will respond to privacy-related inquiries within 30 days.

Your privacy and trust are fundamental to Free Time. We are committed to protecting your data and being transparent about our practices.
