Set up a NFC Sticker

Step 1: Get an NFC Sticker

Purchase NFC Stickers (NTAG215 recommended). They're $8 for a pack of 10 or $12 for a pack of 30. You can share them with friends too.

Buy NFC Stickers on Amazon →

Step 2: Open iOS Shortcuts App

Open the built-in Shortcuts app on your iPhone. Make sure you're on the 'Automations' tab.

Open Shortcuts App →

Step 3: Set up NFC

Tap '+' in top right → Scroll down and select 'NFC' → Tap 'Scan' and hold NFC sticker to your phone. Name it 'Free Time Lock'. → Check 'Run Immediately'

Step 4: Create a New Automation

Tap 'New Blank Automation' under Get Started. Search for 'Open URL' → In the text field, type:

puzzleblock://lock

Step 5: Test It Out

Place your NFC Sticker somewhere where you want to access your apps mindfully (far away from your bed, like on a light switch by your entrance).

Tap the top of your phone to the sticker to start a Digital Detox. Tap it again to end the session.

Pro Tips

  • You can create multiple stickers with the same setup like at your office or friends house.
  • The sticker needs to be touching the top of your phone. If it's not working, try tapping it at a different angle.
