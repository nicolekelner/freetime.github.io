Visualize Your Life in Emojis

Ever wonder how you actually spend your time? We built a simple tool to help you see your life from a new perspective: as a grid of emojis.

Your Life in Emojis takes your daily and weekly activities and calculates how much of your remaining life (from now until age 80) you'll spend on each one. The result? A visual grid where each emoji represents one year of your life.

How it works

Enter your age and adjust the hours you spend on different activities like sleep, work, phone time, exercise, and time with loved ones. The tool instantly calculates and visualizes how those hours add up over a lifetime.

For many people, the results are eye-opening. You might discover that you'll spend more time on your phone than with friends, family, and hobbies combined. Or you might realize just how much of your life goes to sleep (spoiler: it's a lot, and that's okay!).

Why we built this

At Free Time, we believe awareness is the first step to change. Before you can spend your time more intentionally, you need to understand where it's currently going. This tool makes that abstract concept concrete and visual.

Give it a try and see your life in a whole new way.
