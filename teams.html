Why Free Time for Teams?

📈

Boost Productivity

Mindful app blocking helps employees stay focused during work hours by creating intentional pauses before opening distracting apps.

🧘

Support Wellbeing

Help your team build healthier digital habits with breathing exercises, puzzles, and mindfulness activities built into their daily routine.

🤝

Flexible, Not Restrictive

Employees choose their own settings and blocked apps. Free Time creates gentle friction, not punishment — respecting autonomy while encouraging balance.

🚀

Easy Rollout

No MDM or IT infrastructure required. Employees simply download the app on their personal devices and get started in minutes.

Employees Choose What to Block

Each team member selects the apps that distract them most — social media, news, games, or anything else. Free Time puts them in control of their own digital boundaries.

Mindful Pauses, Not Hard Blocks

Before opening a blocked app, employees complete a brief puzzle, breathing exercise, or word scramble. This moment of awareness helps them decide if they truly need the app — reducing mindless scrolling without harsh restrictions.

Focus Schedules and Deep Work Timers

Teams can use scheduled blocking during meetings, focus hours, or deep work sessions. The built-in Pomodoro timer helps employees maintain concentration in productive bursts.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does deployment work?

It's simple — no IT setup or MDM required. We provide your team with a download link or QR code. Employees install Free Time from the App Store on their personal iPhones and configure it to their preferences. We can also provide onboarding materials and a quick-start guide for your team.

Is employee data private?

Absolutely. Free Time is designed with privacy at its core. All app blocking and usage data stays on the employee's device. Employers never have access to individual screen time data or app usage. We don't track, sell, or share personal information.

What devices are supported?

Free Time is currently available for iPhone (iOS). We're working on expanding to additional platforms. The app works on any iPhone running a recent version of iOS.

How is this different from MDM or device management?

Unlike MDM solutions that enforce restrictions from the top down, Free Time empowers employees to manage their own digital habits. There's no surveillance, no forced blocking, and no employer control over personal devices. Employees choose what to block and when — making it a wellness benefit, not a monitoring tool.

Can we try it before committing?

Yes! We offer pilot programs so your team can experience Free Time before a full rollout. Contact us to set up a trial for your organization.
