Lock Distracting Apps

Take control of your screen time by blocking the apps that pull you away from what matters. Free Time helps you stay focused by requiring a mindful pause before opening distracting apps.

Set up custom app groups for social media, games, or any apps you want to use more intentionally. When you try to open a blocked app, you'll be prompted to complete a puzzle or mindfulness activity first.

Build Healthy Phone Habits

Break the cycle of mindless scrolling with puzzles and mindful activities. Each time you want to unlock an app, you'll engage in a brief activity that creates a moment of awareness.

Choose from math puzzles, box breathing exercises, or gratitude journaling. These activities help you pause, reflect, and decide if you really want to use the app right now.

We Never Kick You Off

Unlike aggressive app blockers that force you off apps, Free Time helps you find balance. Once you unlock an app by completing a puzzle, you can use it for your chosen duration without interruption.

You're in control of how long apps stay unlocked - from 5 minutes to several hours. This approach respects your autonomy while encouraging mindful usage.

Track Your Progress

See how much time you're spending on distracting apps and celebrate your progress. Free Time provides insights into your usage patterns without judgment.

Monitor your puzzle completions, unlock frequency, and total focus time. Use these insights to adjust your settings and build even healthier phone habits over time.

Minimalist App Launcher

Reduce compulsive phone checking with a clean, distraction-free app launcher. See only the apps you've chosen to keep accessible, making it easier to use your phone intentionally.

Perfect for creating a focused workspace on your home screen. Launch essential apps without the temptation of notification badges and colorful icons pulling your attention.

Reclaim Your Focus

Set custom schedules for when apps should be automatically blocked. Create different rules for work hours, bedtime, weekends, or any time you need to focus.

Schedule-based blocking helps you establish healthy boundaries with technology. Once your schedule is set, the app works quietly in the background to support your goals.

Simple Pomodoro Timer

Built-in focus timer helps you work in concentrated bursts. Use the Pomodoro technique to maintain deep focus while your distracting apps remain blocked.

Set your work duration, take mindful breaks, and build a sustainable productivity rhythm. The timer integrates seamlessly with your app blocking schedules.
