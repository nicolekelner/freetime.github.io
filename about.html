About the founder

Hi! I'm Nicole Kelner. I'm a full-time artist based in Brooklyn.

I created Free Time because I was feeling hooked on social media when I needed to share my art. I wanted a way to use my phone for work without getting distracted. So I built an app to help me feel more balanced and use my phone with intention.

Free Time just launched this week, and I hope it helps you spend less time on your screen and more time doing what matters most to you.
