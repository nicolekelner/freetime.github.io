Lock Distracting Apps

Take control of your screen time by blocking the apps that pull you away from what matters. Free Time helps you stay focused by requiring a mindful pause before opening distracting apps.

Build Healthy Phone Habits

Break the cycle of mindless scrolling with puzzles and mindful activities. Each time you want to unlock an app, you'll engage in a brief activity that creates a moment of awareness.

We Never Kick You Off

Unlike aggressive app blockers that force you off apps, Free Time helps you find balance. Once you unlock an app by completing a puzzle, you can use it for your chosen duration without interruption.

Minimalist App Launcher

Reduce compulsive phone checking with a clean, distraction-free app launcher. See only the apps you've chosen to keep accessible, making it easier to use your phone intentionally.

Reclaim Your Focus

Set custom schedules for when apps should be automatically blocked. Create different rules for work hours, bedtime, weekends, or any time you need to focus.

Simple Pomodoro Timer

Built-in focus timer helps you work in concentrated bursts. Use the Pomodoro technique to maintain deep focus while your distracting apps remain blocked.
